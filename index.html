Annette Jacoby, Ph.D.

Sociological Research & Data Visualization

As a data visualization specialist, I transform complex research into clear, impactful visual stories. My focus is on urban sociology, wealth disparity, and immigration, and I bring over a decade of experience in translating social data into accessible insights. By bridging rigorous research with creative visualizations, I help organizations and communities better understand and act on critical social issues.
